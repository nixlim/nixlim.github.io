This is Foundry of Zero – a workshop for building things that don’t exist yet. Not incremental improvements, not forks of existing projects, but genuinely new tools forged from scratch with the help of AI coding agents.

What is vibe engineering?

Vibe engineering is a way of building software where the human provides direction, intent, and taste while AI agents handle the implementation details. It’s not about replacing developers – it’s about changing what a developer does. You spend more time thinking about what should exist and why, and less time on the mechanical process of writing every line by hand.

In practice, it looks like this: describe what you want, let the agent build it, review the result, course-correct, and iterate. The vibes are the guiding force – your intuition about what’s right, what’s off, and what needs to change. The agent does the heavy lifting.
